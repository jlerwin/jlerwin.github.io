Latitude vs. Wind Speed


Looks like Chicago is not the only windy city! Check out this graph! This graph looks at wind speed as it relates to latitude. While I don't see much of a relationship there, I do notice some other trends. Also of note, it looks like the most frequently occuring average windspeed is between 0 and 5 miles an hour. No need to hold on to your hats in those cities folks. The cities with the highest wind speeds all seem to be in the northern hemisphere. I wonder why that is!
