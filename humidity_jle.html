Latitude vs. Humidity


"It's not the heat, it's the humidity!" How many times have you heard that one? Well let's take a look at this graph to learn more. There does not appear to be a correlative relationship between latitude and humidty. In other words, I think it might be difficult for us to predict the humidity of a city based on it's proximity to the equator. It does, however, look like there are a few cities in the northern hemisphere who have an average humidity of 0 but none so far that I can see in the southern hemisphere. How fascinating!
